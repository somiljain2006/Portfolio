Smartphone Anaylsis

This code performs a comprehensive analysis of teen smartphone usage by cleaning and preprocessing a dataset, then exploring behavioral patterns through visualizations and statistical correlations. It investigates how screen time—especially before bed—relates to addiction levels, sleep hours, anxiety, and academic performance, highlighting that social media and gaming are major contributors to increased addiction. The analysis also examines demographic factors like gender and school grade, revealing nuanced trends, and concludes with insights into the psychological and lifestyle impacts of excessive smartphone use.
